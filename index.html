Iconography is vital in user interfaces as it enhances visual communication, aids in quick information comprehension, and fosters intuitive navigation. Icons simplify complex concepts, establish brand identity, and contribute to a cohesive and engaging user experience across digital platforms.

Product or display cards are crucial in e-commerce and retail settings as they provide concise and visually appealing information about products, enticing customers to make informed purchasing decisions. These cards showcase product details, key features, pricing, and images in a compact format, enhancing user experience, increasing engagement, and driving conversions.
